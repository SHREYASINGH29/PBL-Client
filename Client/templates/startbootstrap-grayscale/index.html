About Project Based Learning

Now a days with the boom in the field of technology the primitive learning mechanism using chalk and talk is coming to an end. This is where PBL comes into picture. PBL is a well-executed approach for learning new things which incorporates formative feedback, detailed rubrics, multiple evaluations of content and skills.

PBL is a new teaching mechanism which is being implemented so that effective learning can be possible and so that students can learn, execute what they have learnt and understand about the big picture simultaneously

PBL refocuses education on the student, not the curriculum a shift which is necessary to prepare the student to face the real world.

>Base Objectives of PBL are to Identify the Challenge, Craft the Driving Question, Build the Assessment, Plan Blackwards, Enrol and Engage, Faciliate the Terms and Keep the End in Mind.
